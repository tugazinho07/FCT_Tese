# METAGENOMIC BINNING WORKFLOW

Este documento descreve o workflow utilizado para processamento de contigs, concatenação de FASTA, mapping e binning metagenómico com diferentes ferramentas.

---

1. COPIAR CONTIGS

---

cp /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_resultAA/final.contigs.fa 
/home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_resultB/final.contigs.fa 
/home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_resultC/final.contigs.fa 
/home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_3A/final.contigs.fa 
/home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_3B/final.contigs.fa 
/home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_3C/final.contigs.fa 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/

---

2. CONCATENAR FASTA

---

cat /home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227790.prefixed.fa 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227800.prefixed.fa 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227794.prefixed.fa 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227787.prefixed.fa 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227793.prefixed.fa 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227797.prefixed.fa 
> /home/ga.soares/fastas2/concatenated_multisample2.fa

---

3. MAPPING (BOWTIE2 + SAMTOOLS)

---

bowtie2 -x /home/ga.soares/fastas2Mul/multisample_bt2_index 
-1 /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/SRR17227793_1.fastq 
-2 /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/SRR17227793_2.fastq 
-p 32 | 
samtools view -bS - | 
samtools sort -@ 32 
-o /home/ga.soares/fastas2Mul/SRR17227793.multi.sorted.bam

---

4. SEMIBIN2

---

4.1 Concatenação de FASTA:

SemiBin2 concatenate_fasta 
-i /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_resultAA/final.contigs.fa 
/home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_resultB/final.contigs.fa 
/home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_resultC/final.contigs.fa 
-o concatenated_multisample.fa

Alternativa:

SemiBin2 concatenate_fasta -i contig*.fa -o output

4.2 Binning multisample:

SemiBin2 multi_easy_bin 
-i /home/ga.soares/fastas/concatenated_multisample.fa 
-b /home/ga.soares/fastas/SRR17227790.multi.sorted.bam 
/home/ga.soares/fastas/SRR17227800.multi.sorted.bam 
/home/ga.soares/fastas/SRR17227794.multi.sorted.bam 
-o output-mul

4.3 Submissão de job:

oarsub -l "{cluster='charmander'}/nodes=1,walltime=12:30:00" 
-O /home/ga.soares/semibin2_multi.out 
-E /home/ga.soares/semibin2_multi.err 
/home/ga.soares/semibinMul.oar

---

5. METABAT2

---

5.1 Gerar profundidade:

/home/ga.soares/metabat/build/src/jgi_summarize_bam_contig_depths 
--outputDepth depthMUL2.txt 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227787.multi.sorted.bam 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227790.multi.sorted.bam 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227793.multi.sorted.bam 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227794.multi.sorted.bam 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227797.multi.sorted.bam 
/home/ga.soares/fastas2/SRR17227800.multi.sorted.bam

5.2 Executar MetaBAT2:

./metabat2 
-i /home/ga.soares/fastas2/concatenated_multisample2.fa 
-a /home/ga.soares/metabat/build/src/depthMUL2.txt 
-o binsMUL2_dir/bin

5.3 Avaliação com CheckM2:

checkm2 predict 
--threads 30 
--input /home/ga.soares/metabat/build/src/binsMUL2_dir/bin.*.fa 
--output-directory /home/ga.soares/metabat/build/src/binsMUL2_dir/checkm2/

---

6. COMEBIN

---

run_comebin.sh 
-a /home/ga.soares/fastas/concatenated_multisample.fa 
-o /home/ga.soares/ 
-p /home/ga.soares/bam_file/ 
-t 32

---

7. METABINNER

---

7.1 Coverage a partir de FASTQ:

bash /home/ga.soares/ENTER/envs/metabinner/bin/scripts/gen_coverage_file.sh 
-a /home/ga.soares/fastas/concatenated_multisample.fa 
-o /home/ga.soares/CheeseMUL_CoveregePro 
-t 32 
/home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227790_1.fastq 
/home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227790_2.fastq 
/home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227794_1.fastq 
/home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227794_2.fastq 
/home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227800_1.fastq 
/home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227800_2.fastq

7.2 Coverage a partir de BAM:

bash /home/ga.soares/ENTER/envs/metabinner/bin/scripts/gen_coverage_file.sh 
-a /home/ga.soares/fastas/concatenated_multisample.fa 
-o /home/ga.soares/CheeseMUL_CoveregePro 
-t 32 
/home/ga.soares/bamsMULTI/SRR17227790.multi.sorted.bam 
/home/ga.soares/bamsMULTI/SRR17227800.multi.sorted.bam 
/home/ga.soares/bamsMULTI/SRR17227794.multi.sorted.bam

7.3 Submissão de job:

oarsub -l "{cluster='charmander'}/nodes=1,walltime=12:30:00" 
-O /home/ga.soares/CheeseMUL_CoveregePro/metabinner_cov_multi.out 
-E /home/ga.soares/CheeseMUL_CoveregePro/metabinner_cov_multi.err 
/home/ga.soares/metabinner_cov_multi.oar

---

8. CHECKM2 (FINAL)

---

checkm2 predict 
--threads 24 
--input /home/ga.soares/output-mul2/bins/**SemiBin**.fa.gz 
--output-directory /home/ga.soares/output-mul2/checkm2/

---
