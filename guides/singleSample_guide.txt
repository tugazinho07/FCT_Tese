# SINGLE-SAMPLE WORKFLOW (INITIAL VERSION)

Este documento descreve os primeiros testes realizados para assembly, mapping e binning em amostras individuais.

---

1. DOWNLOAD E CONVERSÃO DE DADOS (SRA → FASTQ)

---

fastq-dump --split-files SRR17227790

---

2. ASSEMBLY (MEGAHIT - SINGLE SAMPLE)

---

megahit/bin/megahit 
-1 SRR17227790_1.fastq 
-2 SRR17227790_2.fastq 
-o megahit_result_A

megahit/bin/megahit 
-1 SRR17227800_1.fastq 
-2 SRR17227800_2.fastq 
-o megahit_result_B

megahit/bin/megahit 
-1 SRR17227794_1.fastq 
-2 SRR17227794_2.fastq 
-o megahit_result_C

---

3. MAPPING (BOWTIE2 + SAMTOOLS)

---

3.1 Criar índice:

bowtie2-build 
/home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_A/final.contigs.fa 
/home/ga.soares/contigs_index

3.2 Mapping:

bowtie2 -x /home/ga.soares/contigs_index 
-1 /home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227790_1.fastq 
-2 /home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227790_2.fastq 
--threads 8 
-S alignment.sam

3.3 Converter e ordenar:

samtools view -bS alignment.sam > alignment.bam
samtools sort alignment.bam -o alignment_sorted.bam
samtools index alignment_sorted.bam

---

4. SEMIBIN2

---

4.1 Single-sample binning:

SemiBin2 single_easy_bin 
-i /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_A/final.contigs.fa 
-b /home/ga.soares/alignment_sorted.bam 
-o output 
--environment global

4.2 Multi-sample (teste):

SemiBin2 multi_easy_bin 
-i contig_whole.fa 
-b *.sorted.bam 
-o output

---

5. METABAT2

---

5.1 Gerar ficheiro de profundidade:

/home/ga.soares/metabat/build/src/jgi_summarize_bam_contig_depths 
--outputDepth depth.txt 
/home/ga.soares/alignment_sorted.bam

5.2 Executar MetaBAT2:

./metabat2 
-i /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_A/final.contigs.fa 
-a /home/ga.soares/metabat/build/src/depth.txt 
-o bins_dir/bin

---

6. COMEBIN

---

run_comebin.sh 
-a /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_B/final.contigs.fa 
-o /home/ga.soares/ 
-p /home/ga.soares/bam_files/ 
-t 32

---

7. METABINNER

---

7.1 Gerar coverage:

bash /home/ga.soares/ENTER/envs/metabinner/bin/scripts/gen_coverage_file.sh 
-a /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_C/final.contigs.fa 
-o /home/ga.soares/CheeseC_CoveregePro 
-t 32 
/home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227794_1.fastq 
/home/ga.soares/SRA_Tool/SRR17227794_2.fastq

7.2 Gerar perfil de k-mers:

python gen_kmer.py 
/home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_C/final.contigs.fa 
1000 4

7.3 Executar MetaBinner:

bash run_metabinner.sh 
-a /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_C/final.contigs.fa 
-o /home/ga.soares/CheeseC_CoveregePro/metabinner_res 
-d /home/ga.soares/CheeseC_CoveregePro/coverage_profile_f1k.tsv 
-k /home/ga.soares/MegaHit/megahit_result_C/final.contigs_kmer_4_f1000.csv 
-p /home/ga.soares/ENTER/envs/metabinner/bin

---

8. AVALIAÇÃO (CHECKM2)

---

checkm2 predict 
--threads 30 
--input /home/ga.soares/output7/output_bins/SemiBin_*.fa.gz 
--output-directory /home/ga.soares/output7/checkm2/
